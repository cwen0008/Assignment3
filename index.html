About us

REDAN APARTMENT

Located on a quiet tree lined street in East St Kilda, Redan Apartments is just a short tram ride away from the popular St Kilda beach and cafes, restaurants and boutique shopping that makes St Kilda a sought after location. Alternatively, the cosmopolitan restaurants and shopping of Chapel Street and Toorak Road, as well as Richmond, Prahran and South Eastern suburbs are all close by. REDAN Apartments provides clean and comfortable accommodation, budget prices, with its 30 studio apartments all featuring kitchenette facilities. Tram and train services are within easy walking distance and complimentary secure off street parking, guest laundry and BBQ area is provided for all guests. REDAN Apartments- for the discerning budget conscious traveller.

REDAN APARTMENTS ACCOMMODATION

Redan Apartments is a quality alternative to traditional accommodations, offering guests value for money in a peaceful location. Each apartment is fully furnished complete with a kitchenette. The cooking facilities include 2 hot plates and a microwave, ideal for cooking a simple meal or a snack on the run. The Studio Apartments are generous in size and provide a comfortable space to relax and unwind. A number of the Studio Apartments interconnect to provide an ideal solution for families, sporting groups or couples travelling together.
